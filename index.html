Welcome to ctrlzzz!

Welcome to ctrlzzz! Here you can find all things related to web development, software, the internet, and tech. I'll be writing about projects I'm currently working on as well as cool things I've stumbled upon that have helped me in my career as a software developer as well as in my personal life as a professional nerd.


A few things about me... I recently finished a Bachelor's degree in IT and I've spent the last few years working on learning tech and developing projects. Most recenty, I finished a flight price tracking tool to help me buy cheap flights. The program grabs prices from QPX Express (Google Flights API), processes them with a Java backend, and stores results in a relational database using MySQL.
